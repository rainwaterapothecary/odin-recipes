Spaghetti with Cream Sauce

Here's a creamy tomato sauce recipe given to me by my dear friend Darrell.

I remember making this dish numerous times after he gave it to me with my friend Wren. We would add different types of hot peppers to add spice but I have included the base recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 Jar Prego or other pre-made spaghetti sauce
  • Minced garlic
  • Kosher salt & black pepper to taste
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Italian Herb blend
  • Butter optional

Steps

  1. In a saucepan combine heavy whipping cream with pre-made sauce. It should come out a bright orange color.
  2. Turn stovetop to medium heat, stirring regularly.
  3. Add garlic, herbs, red pepper flakes, and salt and pepper. Taste as you go and add more of each ingredient as you wish.
  4. Serve over spaghetti with fresh parmesan cheese sprinkled over it.
  5. Enjoy!
